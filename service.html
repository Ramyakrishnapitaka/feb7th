The World Bank EdStats Portal (Education Statistics Portal) is a platform to allow for easy access to data for key indicators in education covering topics such as access, completion, learning outcomes, expenditures, policy, and equity. EdStats is currently being revamped to improve user experience and relevance so certain indicators may not be up to date. The EdStats dataset as well as other education datasets remain accessible in the meantime through the links below. Explore EdStats indicators and download EdStats database levels from pre-primary to tertiary education. Download other Education datasets such as the Learning Poverty database, the Harmonized Learning Outcomes database, and the Country Learning Outcomes (CLO) of harmonized learning assessments
